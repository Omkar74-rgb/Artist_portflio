About Me

I, Pooja Ashok Palsambkar, was born in 1997, at Villeparle - Mumbai - Maharashtra, India. From childhood I have always been fascinated by drawing and painting. After finishing schooling, my interest was cultivated during my teenage years and further developed when I opted to take my masterâ€™s degree in Portraiture. I studied painting at SNDT Women's University, Mumbai in 2021.

After completing my studies in 2021, I delved even more into my art practice. Portraiture is always a challenging and fascinating subject for me, and I love to create all those emotions in my paintings. I felt a strong connection with people, and I painted day-to-day life that always surrounded me and all those movements I depicted into my canvases.

In the initial stages, only Representational Art got deep rooted in my being. For some period, I worked in the Academic style and created Landscapes, still life and portraits. Portrait painting is my favorite subject and my profession also. Then I started painting on Figurative compositions. Most of my work is in oil painting medium. While doing this, the space division and a different approach to composition started getting important in my paintings. Human beings are an immaculate and essential part of nature which inspires me most. They have appealed to me to paint them relentlessly.

My works are related to day to day life. I believe that such simple day to day situations provide me with the germ of ideas. I enjoy observing people in different moods, their gestures, postures & feelings. The connection between me and them. As soon as I get connected to a subject and it touches my inner core, it naturally comes into my art work. I received some prestigious achievements right from various Art Institutions and Art Societies in India. These paintings fetched me awards in the Professional category.

One of the paintings was awarded by the Maharashtra state Art competition and The Bombay Art Society. I also received the CCRT Government Young Artist Scholarship. There were Held exhibitions in the Art Society of India. This style acclaimed awards from other States also. I am still working on the same subject to emphasize the different perspectives of Human beings.
